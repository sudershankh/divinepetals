Brand Story

Made by hand, with everything nature intended

Divine Petals began in a small kitchen in 2016, with a mortar, pestle, and a deep love for botanicals. What started as a personal quest for clean skincare became something bigger — a community of people who believe that what we put on our skin matters as much as what we put in our body.

Every bar is cold-processed in small batches to preserve the natural glycerin and botanical benefits. We cure each soap for a minimum of six weeks — no shortcuts, no rush.
